PRIVACY POLICY FOR KDS VR APP
KDS VR is an application developed by Visioment Studios. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to complying with all applicable privacy regulations, including the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

COMMITMENT TO CONFIDENTIALITY
Visioment Studios is committed to safeguarding your privacy while you use the KDS VR app. Below are the key points of our privacy policy:

Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" refers to any data or information exchanged between Visioment Studios and the user in any form. However, within the context of this app, no such exchange takes place. We guarantee that no data is collected, shared, or transmitted.

All information related to your use of the app remains entirely private. KDS VR operates entirely offline, ensuring that no data is transmitted, sold, or monetized. No third-party providers will have access to any information.

Duration of the Agreement
This privacy agreement is effective from the moment you download and install the KDS VR app. The terms of this agreement will remain in effect as long as you use the app. Since no data is collected, no further actions are required on your part.

Governing Law and Jurisdiction
This privacy policy is governed by the laws applicable in your country of residence. Any disputes related to this policy will be handled under the relevant legal jurisdictions.

USER RIGHTS & DATA PROTECTION
As KDS VR does not collect, store, or process any personal data, you maintain full control over your information. There are no user rights to exercise in relation to personal data, as we do not request or store any such information.

DATA TRANSMISSION
There is no data transmission involved in using KDS VR. The app is fully offline and does not require or request an internet connection. As a result, no data, including personal or gameplay-related details, is shared or transmitted.

HOW WE PROTECT YOUR PRIVACY
Visioment Studios is dedicated to ensuring your privacy throughout your experience with KDS VR. Specifically, we guarantee that:

KDS VR is an offline application.
No data, including personal or statistical details, is collected.
